The Incredible Super Build Project

We are a group of students with the help of two facilitators, using our ASA time to build this project.

What are we building?
We are currently designing and building a 20 meter long roller coaster to be finished and ridable at the ISB Innovation EXPO at November 25th.

How can I help?
We're so happy you asked! There are many ways you can help. Either joining us after school on Wednesdays or Fridays in the MS-HS Design Lab 3116, or by volunteering your time on weekends when we are building it!
